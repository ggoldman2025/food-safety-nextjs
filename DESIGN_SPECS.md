# Professional Design Specifications

Based on analysis of Stripe and other top SaaS sites:

## Spacing Standards

### Navigation
- Height: 80px (h-20)
- Horizontal padding: 24-32px (px-6 lg:px-8)
- Logo + text size: 32px (text-2xl)
- Button padding: 12px 24px (px-6 py-3)

### Hero Section
- Top padding: 120-160px (pt-32 lg:pt-40)
- Bottom padding: 120-160px (pb-32 lg:pb-40)
- Max width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Headline size: 72-96px (text-7xl lg:text-8xl)
- Subheadline size: 24-28px (text-2xl lg:text-3xl)
- Line height: 1.1-1.2 for headlines
- Margin between headline and subheadline: 32px (mb-8)
- Margin below subheadline: 48px (mb-12)

### Content Sections
- Vertical padding: 128-160px (py-32 lg:py-40)
- Section heading margin bottom: 96px (mb-24)
- Max content width: 1280px (max-w-7xl)
- Card padding: 48-64px (p-12 lg:p-16)
- Gap between cards: 32px (gap-8)

### Typography
- Hero headline: 72-96px, font-bold, line-height 1.1
- Section headings: 48-60px, font-bold
- Card headings: 28-32px, font-bold
- Body text: 18-20px, line-height 1.6-1.8
- Small text: 14-16px

### Colors & Effects
- Background: Gradient with multiple stops
- Cards: bg-white/5 with backdrop-blur
- Borders: border-white/10
- Hover states: bg-white/10, scale-105
- Shadows: shadow-2xl for CTAs

## Key Principles
1. GENEROUS whitespace - don't be afraid of empty space
2. Clear visual hierarchy with size and weight
3. Consistent spacing rhythm (multiples of 8px)
4. Centered content with max-width containers
5. Subtle transparency and blur effects
6. Smooth transitions and hover states
